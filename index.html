Workflow gap analysis

Gap analyses revealed $7500 is wasted everyday in idle worktime in an FMCG Company of over 1,500 employees. As this leakage arose from extra 45 minutes after breaktime queuing for food, we automated a food ordering system that curbed this loss & improved employee's satisfaction.

Excel superstore
